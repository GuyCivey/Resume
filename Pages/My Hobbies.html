My Story

I began my web development journey in 2020 during the pandemic, when I found myself out of work and eager to learn new skills. I started by teaching myself HTML, which quickly evolved into learning CSS and JavaScript. As my knowledge grew, I pursued formal lessons to further deepen my understanding of web development. Although I was able to pick up the material quickly, my time to practice became limited once I returned to work.

As my family grew, I saw new opportunities in applying my skills to smart home technologies. This shift in focus allowed me to use my coding knowledge in a practical setting, further developing my skillset. However, over time, my focus shifted away from general coding and programming and into proprietary systems that were limited to specific applications. Realizing I wanted to return to my passion for coding, I began searching for a career path that would allow me to fully dedicate myself to web development and continue growing as a developer.
